Beau Holliday is an American musician, writer, romance author, and poet’s presence on the web and social media, with obsession for the romantic and sensual, exploring the psychological and spiritual aspects of sex, immersing himself into the history and mysticism of desire, and drawing on all of it to build psuedo-fictional fantasies, academic pursuits, and philosophical pondering within unique and intriguing artistic endeavors online, and off.
