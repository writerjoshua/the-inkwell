# Welcome to The Inkwell's Prompt Collection!

You've read me.
Now, I want to read you.

When the time feels right, I will offer you a thought, a word, or a sentiment.
Use the form to submit your response, in any format you like.

Send me your poetry, short stories, or just strong feelings about the prompt.
Feel free to write anonymously but, with your permission, I will share my favorites.

Email: write@beauholliday.com

So, introduce yourself.
Tell me what made you come.
