Steady
A love
The world tests
Pull and push
Angle and chase
No, thanks
Digital bias
Spiritual warp
But so centered 
All flows around
This love
Sound
Yes, please 
Resound and reform
Integrity borne 
Holding our ground 
Just for
Love
